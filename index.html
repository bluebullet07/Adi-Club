About us

Thanks for visiting our website which provides you with all you need to know about our club.

Adidda Club is a medium sized sports and social/competitive club based at Peterborough, Cambridgeshire. It's important for us to be involved in local sporting initiatives and to be easily accessible to people in and around Peterborough that want to exercise, socialise and compete.

Currently we play Badminton and Cricket, And the club is affiliated to the Badminton Association of England for Badminton and Affiliated to England and Wales cricket board(ECB) for Cricket. We also organise a number of social events throughout the year.

Cricket League

Adidda Cricket club is affiliated to ECB, and we play in Huntingdonshire county cricket league. we provide cricket for seniors and are in process of developing teams for juniors.

For the 2017 season the club entered into the Huntingdonshire (Hunts) County Cricket League Division 5. The club found their temporary home in Orton Bushfields Vivacity centre where we have entered a ground-sharing arrangement with Vivacity. Orton Bushfield is well known for its friendly atmosphere with all teams receiving a warm welcome. In 2017 season we finished runners up in the HCCL division 5. And for 2018 season we have prompted to HCCL division 4 playing on Saturdays.

Weekly net practice sessions

We do our netts at Peterborough Town cricket netts on Saturday Mornings between 7.00AM and 10:00AM, which starts from Mid of March.

New Players are always welcome, if you are interested in joining please feel free to contact through email adiddaclub@gmail.com.

Badminton

Gathering a wide variety of people together with rackets and shuttles on badminton courts naturally produces energy and enjoyment. That, on its own, is a good enough reason to run a club.

We are an established, friendly badminton club, who always welcome new members and visitors alike. We enjoy the game the way it should be played, with nylon shuttles in a competitive, friendly and relaxed atmosphere.

Weekly Badminton sessions

We play every Friday night from 20:00 – 22:00 at Hampton Vivacity. As an active and friendly Badminton Club, we are always keen to welcome visitors and new members.

  • 8.00pm - 9.00pm on Friday’s
  • 3 Courts
  • Nylon Shuttles
  • Fun & Competitive
  • Friendly & Relaxed
  • Visitors Welcome
  • Intermediate Level
  • Reduced Fees for Juniors
  • First Session is Free for New Members

We develop players through coaching and competition, many badminton players want to improve and so we will continue to provide access to qualified and experienced coaches. Improvement can also come from playing with and against more experienced players in competitive environments.

The club caters for league standard players and we have a number of competitive teams playing in the Men’s division of Hunts and Peterborough Leagues. The Home games and League players practice games are played on Thursday’s between 7.00pm – 9.00pm.
